What Are You Getting Out of a Pergola?

Pergolas are nothing new. They've been a staple in many homes all over the world for centuries now. But what exactly are they? What do they do? And lastly, what can you get out of them?

A pergola is an outdoor structure that provides shade and protection from the elements. They're typically constructed from wood or metal and typically have an open lattice roof. This allows for air and light to pass through while still providing some protection from the sun and rain.
